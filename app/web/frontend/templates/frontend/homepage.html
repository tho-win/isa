Welcome to UVA SwipeMe!
An application that allows students at University of Virginia to make the best use of their remaining
meal swipes by selling them to other fellow students.

Have you ever been bothered by the extra meal swipes left in your meal plan at the end of semester?
Have you ever run out of meal swipes near the end of semester and have to use credit card to pay more money to get into the dinning hall?

If you used to have those experience, then this app is for YOU!
On this app, you can save some money, make some extra cash and help other fellow students out!
